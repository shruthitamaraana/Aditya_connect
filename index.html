About Aditya Connect

Bridging the gap between campus communities through seamless digital connectivity

Aditya Connect is the premier digital platform designed specifically for our university community. We bring together students, faculty, and staff in a unified ecosystem that promotes collaboration, information sharing, and academic excellence.


Built with modern technology including React.js frontend, Node.js backend, MongoDB database, and secure Firebase authentication, our platform ensures reliability, security, and seamless user experience across all devices.
