Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

Where can I find placement updates?

Placement updates are available in the "Quick Access" section on the dashboard. You can also navigate to the dedicated Placements page from the main menu. Here you'll find job opportunities, internship listings, company visit schedules, and placement statistics. Make sure to enable notifications to stay updated with the latest opportunities.

How do I submit an article?

Article submission depends on your role. Students can submit articles through the "Student Blog" section, while faculty members can use the "Research" section. Navigate to the appropriate section, click "Submit New Article," and fill out the required information including title, content, and relevant tags. Your submission will be reviewed before publication.

How do I update my profile information?

Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Profile Settings." Here you can update your personal information, contact details, profile picture, and notification preferences. Some fields may require admin approval for changes, especially academic information.

Why can't I access certain features?

Feature access is role-based. Students, faculty, and admin users have different permissions. If you believe you should have access to a specific feature, contact your system administrator or use the contact form below to request access.

How do I report a technical issue?

You can report technical issues using the contact form below or by emailing support@adityaconnect.in. Please include details about the issue, steps to reproduce it, your browser information, and any error messages you received.
