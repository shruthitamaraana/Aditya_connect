Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aditya Connect, the educational web platform designed for students, faculty, and staff of Aditya Educational Institutions. Aditya Connect serves as a centralized hub for academic collaboration, communication, and resource sharing within our educational community.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform.

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and will only collect and use information that is necessary to provide you with the best educational experience possible.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our educational services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Information:

  • Basic Profile Data: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Academic Information: Department, course, year of study, student/faculty ID
  • Role Information: Whether you are a student, faculty member, or administrative staff
  • Profile Picture: Optional profile image for identification purposes

Activity and Interaction Data:

  • Posts and Comments: Content you create, share, or comment on
  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, shares, and other interactions with platform content
  • Communication Logs: Messages sent through our platform (for quality and safety purposes)
  • Learning Progress: Assignment submissions, grades, and academic progress tracking

Technical Information:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device model
  • Usage Data: Login times, pages visited, time spent on platform
  • IP Address: For security and geographic insights

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Educational Services: To provide personalized learning experiences, course recommendations, and academic support
  • Communication: To send important updates, announcements, and notifications related to your courses and the institution
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform's functionality and user experience
  • Academic Progress Tracking: To monitor and report on your academic progress to relevant faculty and administrators
  • Community Building: To facilitate connections between students and faculty with similar academic interests
  • Security and Safety: To maintain a safe learning environment and prevent misuse of the platform
  • Technical Support: To provide customer support and resolve technical issues

We will never use your information for unauthorized commercial purposes or share it with third parties for marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Data Security and Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Databases: Your information is stored in secure MongoDB databases with encrypted storage
  • Firebase Security: We utilize Google Firebase's robust security infrastructure for authentication and data storage
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data

Operational Security:

  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
  • Staff Training: Our team is trained on data protection best practices
  • Incident Response: We have procedures in place to quickly respond to any security incidents
Important Note: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and report any suspicious activity immediately.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Aditya Connect uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience:

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality, including login sessions and security features
  • Authentication Cookies: To keep you logged in and maintain your session across different pages
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings, language preferences, and customization choices
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how you use our platform and improve our services (anonymized data only)

Managing Cookies:

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of Aditya Connect.

Session Management: We use secure session tokens to maintain your login status and ensure you don't need to repeatedly authenticate during your session.

6. Third-party Services and Integrations

To provide comprehensive educational services, Aditya Connect integrates with several trusted third-party services:

Authentication Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Google's Firebase service handles user authentication and account security
  • OAuth Integration: Support for Google and Microsoft account login for institutional email addresses
  • Single Sign-On (SSO): Integration with institutional identity management systems

Data Storage and Processing:

  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database service for secure data storage and management
  • Google Cloud Services: For additional storage and processing capabilities
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): For faster content delivery and improved performance

Data Protection: All third-party services we use are required to maintain appropriate data protection standards and comply with relevant privacy regulations. We have data processing agreements in place with all service providers.

External Links: Our platform may contain links to external educational resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

As a user of Aditya Connect, you have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Transparency:

  • Data Access: You can request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Activity Log: View your account activity and interaction history through your profile settings

Control and Modification:

  • Profile Updates: You can edit your profile information, preferences, and privacy settings at any time
  • Content Management: Delete or modify posts, comments, and other content you've created
  • Communication Preferences: Choose which notifications and updates you want to receive

Data Removal:

  • Account Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and associated data
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific pieces of information while maintaining your account
  • Right to be Forgotten: Request removal of your data from our systems (subject to legal and academic record requirements)

Important Note: Some information may need to be retained for academic record-keeping, legal compliance, or institutional requirements even after account deletion.

  • Allow a reasonable time period for review before the changes take effect
